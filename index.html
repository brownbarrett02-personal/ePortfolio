Howdy!

My name is Barrett Brown, and I'm a senior Aerospace Engineering and Political Science Major at Texas A&M University, with minors in Astrophysics and Mathematics.

This portfolio reflects both sides of who I am: a technically rigorous engineer who cares deeply about the public dimensions of science and technology. I believe that the most impactful advocates for better policy are people who have done the work — who have run the simulations, built the hardware, and understood the constraints from the inside.

Before I transition into science policy, I want to spend meaningful time as a practicing engineer. That commitment drives my research in the Aerospace Materials Lab, my internships at Southwest Research Institute and Bray International, Inc., and my continued pursuit of depth across multiple technical disciplines.
